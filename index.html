FAQ


EagleHacks is a 200 person, 24-hour hackathon for high schoolers in the Bay Area, September 14-15, 2019 at 42 Silicon Valley.

Any high school student can participate. If you've graduated from high school, you can apply to be a mentor(see above) If you're in middle school, you may be able to participate, please email us at eaglehacks2019@gmail.com.

That’s perfectly okay! We have activities lined up that do not involve any code. Interested in learning code? We will have coding activities designed for those who don’t have coding experience.

We will have a team mixer activity from 11am to noon where you can get to know new people and create a team. You are also welcome to work alone if you so choose.

Eaglehacks is completely free! We'll be providing food, workspace, and swag all free of cost! 42SV is also allowing attendees to use the Macs they have in the venue.

Please bring your school id! You will not be allowed into the venue without it. You should also bring a sleeping bag, a toothbrush, toothpaste, and anything that will make your stay more comfortable and hygienic. Please do not bring anything that violates our Code of Conduct (see https://eaglehacks2019.devpost.com/rules)

Please let us know on the sign-up form or email us. We are happy to accommodate most dietary restrictions.

Email us! We’ll be happy to answer any and all of your questions. Our email is eaglehacks2019@gmail.com
